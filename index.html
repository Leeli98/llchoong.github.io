Welcome to Green Acres!

  • ABOUT US
    •

    Our family-owned and operated farm located in the beautiful countryside of rural America. Our farm has been providing fresh and organic produce to the local community for over three generations. We take pride in our sustainable farming practices and strive to be good stewards of the land.

    • Interact with Goats
      •

      At our farm, we offer a unique opportunity to participate in feeding our adorable baby goats! Visitors can experience the joy of bottle-feeding these curious and playful animals, and learn about the special care and attention they require in their early stages of life. Our experienced staff will provide guidance on how to safely handle the baby goats, and answer any questions you may have about their behavior and development. It's a fun and educational activity that's perfect for families, animal lovers, and anyone who wants to get up close and personal with these lovable creatures. Come join us for this unforgettable experience!

    • Get Fresh Tomatoes
      •

      At our farm, you can experience the joy of picking your own fresh, juicy tomatoes straight from the vine! Our tomato fields are open to the public during the picking season, and visitors are invited to come and harvest their own ripe and flavorful tomatoes. We provide baskets and all the necessary equipment for picking, and our friendly staff will guide you through the fields and offer tips on finding the best tomatoes. It's a fun and wholesome activity for the whole family, and a great way to experience the beauty of nature while enjoying some tasty treats. So come join us for a day of tomato picking and create memories that will last a lifetime!

    • Pony Rides
      •

      Our farm offers pony riding for all ages and experience levels! Our gentle and well-trained ponies are perfect for kids and adults alike, and our experienced staff will ensure a safe and enjoyable ride for everyone. We have a variety of routes and trails to choose from, ranging from short rides for beginners to longer routes for more experienced riders. Whether you want to explore the beautiful countryside or simply enjoy a leisurely ride around our farm, we've got you covered. So come saddle up and join us for an unforgettable pony riding experience!

    • U Pick Strawberry
      •

      There's nothing quite like the taste of freshly picked strawberries, and at our farm, you can experience the joy of picking your own! Our strawberry fields are open to the public during the picking season, and visitors are invited to come and pick their own delicious berries straight from the vine. We provide baskets for picking, and our friendly staff will guide you through the fields and offer tips on finding the sweetest and ripest berries. It's a fun and wholesome activity for the whole family, and a great way to experience the beauty of nature while enjoying some tasty treats. So come join us for a day of strawberry picking and create memories that will last a lifetime!
